About Lucknow SafeZone

Lucknow SafeZone is a free public service initiative for the citizens of Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.

Our Mission

To provide real-time Air Quality Index data, emergency contact numbers, and women safety resources at one place so citizens can stay informed and safe.

Data Source

AQI data is fetched from public APIs. Emergency numbers are as per Government of India guidelines.
